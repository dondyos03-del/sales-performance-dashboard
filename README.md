# Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an interactive sales dashboard built using Power BI to analyze business performance across time, regions, and product categories.

---

## Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard](sales-db.png)

---
## Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, including:

- Sales trends over time
- Regional performance analysis
- Product category distribution
- Top-performing products
- Shipping efficiency insights

---

## Key Insights

- Sales show an overall upward trend with noticeable fluctuations and periodic spikes.
- The West region leads overall performance, while the South shows lower contribution.
- Technology is the top-performing category, generating the highest revenue.
- Sales are concentrated in a small number of products, indicating dependency on key items.
- Shipping time varies slightly at the state level, typically ranging between 4 and 5 days, while remaining overall consistent.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve performance in poor-performing regions, such as the South, through targeted marketing and distribution strategies.
- Reduce dependency on top-performing products by diversifying the product portfolio.
- Invest further in the Technology category to leverage its strong performance.
- Maintain current shipping efficiency while exploring opportunities to further optimize delivery time.

## Data Source
This dataset was obtained from Kaggle and represents sample sales data designed to simulate real-world business scenarios.

It includes information such as order dates, product categories, regional performance, and shipping details, and was used to analyze trends and extract business insights.

Dataset Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohitsahoo/sales-forecasting
